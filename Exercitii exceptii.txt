Bank Account System with OOP and Exceptions:


You are building a simple banking system.

The system must support:

creating bank accounts

depositing money

withdrawing money

preventing invalid operations

Errors must be handled using custom exceptions.

Create the following classes: 
BankAccount
SavingsAccount
InvalidAmountException
InsufficientFundsException
BankApp (main program)

Create methods that  respond to this necessities: 

creating bank accounts

depositing money

withdrawing money

preventing invalid operations

Create custom exception classes for at least 2 of the scenarios above.
